Features

Everything your business needs
to stay tax-compliant

From PAYE calculations to AI-powered document scanning — TaxWise covers the full Nigerian tax compliance lifecycle.

🧮

PAYE & CIT Calculator

Automatically compute monthly Personal Income Tax (PAYE) for any number of employees using FIRS tax bands and all applicable reliefs.

📊

VAT & WHT Reporting

Generate FIRS-standard VAT and Withholding Tax reports. Track input/output VAT, compute net payable, and export ready-to-file returns.

🤖

Document AI Scanner

Upload invoices, receipts, or bank statements. AI extracts the vendor, amount, date, and category — and creates journal entries automatically.

👥

Team Management

Invite accountants, cashiers, and managers with granular role-based permissions. Track activity logs and manage access from one dashboard.

🏦

Bank Reconciliation

Connect your Nigerian bank accounts via Mono. Import transactions automatically, match them to entries, and flag unreconciled items instantly.

⏰

Tax Deadline Reminders

Never miss a FIRS filing deadline again. Set custom reminders for VAT, PAYE, WHT, and CIT due dates with desktop notifications.
